Paris


Paris, France's capital, is a major European city and a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy and culture. Its 19th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by wide boulevards and the River Seine. Beyond such landmarks as the Eiffel Tower and the 12th-century, Gothic Notre-Dame cathedral, the city is known for its cafe culture and designer boutiques

  1. The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France
  2. The Louvre, or the Louvre Museum, is a national art museum in Paris, France
  3. Notre-Dame de Paris aka Notre-Dame, is a medieval Catholic cathedral in the 4th arrondissement of Paris
  4. Arc de Triomphe de l'Étoile akaArc de Triomphe , is one of the most famous monuments in Paris
  5. Musée d'Orsay is a museum in Paris, France, on the Left Bank of the Seine
